Ignore words

Ignore words are words that you want to treat a little bit differently. Ignore words:

    Do not show up in the main lexicon search
    Are not suggested by spell check to correct spelling errors
    But do not cause a spelling error when spelt correctly

You may decide for example that Proper nouns shouldn't display when searching the Lexicon, but should be included in the spell check.
